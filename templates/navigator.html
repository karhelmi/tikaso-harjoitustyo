Good To Know About The App

Welcome to use the app! The purpose of this app is to support an entity in product improvement by providing a platform for product users to share their ideas for product improvement, initiate product improvement projects based on the raised ideas and monitor all this.

The app structure is explained and few links provided below (after you have now logged in).

  1. Products (View a list of our products)
    • Select a product and share you improvement idea
    • Select a product and view the raised ideas for that product
  2. Ideas (View a list of all the raised ideas)
  3. Initiate a project (based on a raised idea)
    • Select an idea and initiate a project by adding team name & team members for it.
  4. Projects (View a list of initiated projects and related information)
    • View project team members for each individual project team (through hyperlinks).
    • List of employees involved in improvement projects
  5. To logout - use the button on the navigation bar.
